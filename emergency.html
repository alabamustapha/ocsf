Emergency Aid

We at orphanage care and support foundation occassionaly offers emergency aids in form of health care services, distribution of cash and raw food pack and house renovation. We also provide vulnerable families white cloth for Janaza burial at community mosque and wood for burials at cemetery.

Our Impact

Khadija an orphan female student of the orphan free school Badikko discussed with her counsellor during a trauma recovery session. She explained that she, her sisters and mother often face threat of rape at midnights due to the fact that their room does not have a secured door or wall. For the safety of the orphans and their widowed mother, the organization renovated their house

A needy woman pleads for help: She can’t trace her husband due to insecurity in the Northern part of the country. She is left with two daughters and she can’t care for them due to her condition. She is a sickle cell patient and also diagnosed with cancer. One of her legs has a big sore which isn’t healing, She presently limb while walking as she is tired and weak. She also suffers severe psychological trauma. The organization has taken up her hospital expenses, provided her accommodation, monthly stipend and monthly food pack support.
