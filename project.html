Orphans Free School Project

On Oct 6, 2018 Orphan free school was established to cater for the primary needs of orphans of Badiko community Kaduna Nigeria. The free school initially enrolled and provides for 120 orphans but presently has 200 orphans between the ages of 4-15. The school program runs Monday through Friday delivering Western and Islamic education, vocational skills training, IT and trauma recovery to the children n need.

Our Mission

Alongside the free education, Orphanage Care and Support Foundation also provides hot meals on a daily basis for all the students and transportation for the students making their journey to school and home easy and safe. The orphans also benefit from free school uniform set, socks, sandals and free educational materials. Summaringly, the Orphans free school project provides the following to orphans:

. Free education

. Free meals

. Free school uniform and kit

. Free medicals

. Free skills training
